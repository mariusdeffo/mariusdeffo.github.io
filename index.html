About

I am a Ph.D. student and Teaching Assistant in the Rutgers French Department. I hold a combined honours bachelor's degree in English and French and an MA in the Teaching of French as a Foreign Language from the University of Buea. My interdisciplinary research spans sociolinguistics, didactics, linguistics, and postcolonial studies.

  • Institution: Rutgers University — French Department
  • Alma mater: University of Buea
  • Current project: Thesis on colonial trauma
  • Interests: Oral literature, migration, disasters, trauma, diasporic studies

Research & Current Projects

Selected projects and themes:

  1. Repenser le contexte et la géographie du trauma colonial dans la littérature francophone.
  2. Investigations en sociolinguistique et didactique du français langue étrangère.
  3. Oral literature and memory in Francophone Africa.
