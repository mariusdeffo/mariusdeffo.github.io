About Me

Marius Kahakeu Deffo

I am a Ph.D. student and Teaching Assistant in the Rutgers French Department. I hold a combined honours bachelor's degree in English and French from the University of Buea. I hold an MA in The Teaching of French as a Foreign Language at the same institution. My fields of research include Sociolinguistics, Didactics, and linguistics. I am also passionate about colonial and postcolonial studies. I intend to pursue interdisciplinary research on the Francophone world's culture and literature. I am also interested in oral literature, migration, disasters, trauma, and diasporic studies. I am currently working on my thesis project focusing on colonial trauma.
