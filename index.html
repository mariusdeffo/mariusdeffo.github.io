About Me

I am a Ph.D. student and Teaching Assistant in the Rutgers French Department. I hold a combined honours bachelor's degree in English and French from University of Buea and an MA in The Teaching of French as a Foreign Language.

My research focuses on Sociolinguistics, Didactics, linguistics, colonial & postcolonial studies, oral literature, migration, disasters, trauma, and diasporic studies. I am currently working on my thesis project on colonial trauma.
